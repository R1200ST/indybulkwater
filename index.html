Vinyl liner pool owners - read this first
Never empty a vinyl liner pool without having a delivery truck scheduled for the same day. Vinyl liners shrink when exposed to air and will not stretch back to full size. Once you have cleaned the pool, put at least 4 inches of water back in immediately to keep the liner pressed against the wall.
